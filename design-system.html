RULES / INSTRUCTIONS

HOW TO PLAY

1. Click the balloon to pump it up and earn money.
2. Each pump adds Rp 500 but increases pop risk.
3. Click SAVE to bank your earnings before it pops.
4. If it pops, you lose all earnings for that round.

SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS

Camera access required. Browser must support Visibility API. Fullscreen mode will be activated during the assessment.
